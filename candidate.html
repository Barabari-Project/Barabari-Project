Get Mentorship Be a Mentor Join Us! Meet the Team

The Barabari Project is a non-profit working to provide quality training and mentorship to candidates from low-income categories, rural backgrounds, underprivileged caste sections, and underrepresented gender backgrounds. We want to become a gateway for candidates who lack access to these opportunities, to up-skill themselves, get well paying jobs, and become self-sufficient to take ownership of their careers.
